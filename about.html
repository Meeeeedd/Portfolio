My Journey: From Concept to Code

As a dynamic and results-driven Software Engineering Degree student at ESPRIM, building upon a solid foundation from my Bachelor's in Software Engineering (ISIMA), I thrive on transforming complex technical challenges into innovative, scalable, and high-impact software solutions. My journey is defined by a relentless pursuit of innovation and a deep commitment to mastering the intricate dance between front-end aesthetics and robust back-end logic.

My expertise encompasses full-stack web development, particularly with the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js), crafting intuitive mobile applications with React Native, and engineering robust desktop solutions using JavaFX. I've spearheaded the development of key projects, including the comprehensive e-commerce platform "HERMES" and the versatile multi-platform application "MIRAVIASPACE", a collaborative achievement with my team, Innosynergy, which was nominated at Bal des Projets and secured 2nd place.

My passion for cutting-edge technology is further demonstrated by co-creating "BinGoBin," an AI-powered smart sorting bin leveraging IoT in the "Greenovation" Hackathon at ESPRIM in December 2024, and developing "BioUnit," a waste-to-energy system that secured 2nd Place in the "Clean & Green" Hackathon at ESPRIT in 2025. These experiences have significantly sharpened my skills in Artificial Intelligence, IoT, rapid prototyping, and agile, collaborative problem-solving. Fluent in JavaScript, PHP, Java, Python, and C++, I am deeply committed to mastering emerging technologies and contributing effectively to all phases of the Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC). My clear ambition is to evolve into a Tech Lead, guiding and inspiring teams to achieve technical excellence and deliver transformative digital products.
