My Journey: From Concept to Code

As a dedicated full-stack developer and an engineering student at ESPRIM, my passion lies in transforming complex ideas into elegant, high-performance digital realities. My journey is defined by a relentless pursuit of innovation and a deep commitment to mastering the intricate dance between front-end aesthetics and robust back-end logic.

I thrive on building comprehensive solutions, from the dynamic user interfaces crafted with the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js) to the powerful server-side architectures developed with Symfony and Spring Boot. My expertise extends to multi-platform applications like JavaFX desktop clients, and I leverage Docker for seamless deployment and Arduino for exciting IoT integrations, demonstrating a versatile command across the entire development spectrum.

My work, exemplified by projects like the robust Hermes e-commerce platform, the multi-faceted MIRAVIASPACE travel & social hub (a project nominated at Bal des Projets and securing 2nd place), and the innovative SAFENEST smart home mobile app, showcases my ability to deliver impactful and scalable products. I'm a proven problem-solver and a collaborative team player, honed through numerous hackathons, including the "Greenovation" and "Clean & Green" challenges where I secured second place.

Beyond the screen, my interests in kick-boxing, chess, and reading reflect a dedication to discipline, strategic thinking, and continuous self-improvement – qualities I bring to every line of code. I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to contribute to large-scale, transformative projects, with an unwavering vision to lead and inspire as a future Tech Lead.
